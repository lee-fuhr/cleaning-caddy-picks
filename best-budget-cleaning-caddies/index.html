A good cleaning caddy turns a pile of spray bottles, sponges, and scrub brushes into a portable command center. Whether you’re carrying supplies from room to room or just trying to tame the clutter under your sink, the right caddy saves time and frustration. Budget options used to feel flimsy, but the market has improved dramatically, now you can find tough, well-designed carriers without emptying your wallet. The key is to look for a caddy that balances capacity with maneuverability. Avoid anything so large it becomes unwieldy when full, and check that the handle and base are molded in one piece (seams are weak points). Also consider whether you need a tote for carrying or a more stationary organizer for under-sink storage.
