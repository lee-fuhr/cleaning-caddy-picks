Under-sink cabinets are notorious for becoming a black hole of spray bottles, sponges, and mystery drips. A good organizer turns that chaos into a tidy, accessible system, and the right one can save you time and frustration during every cleaning session. Material matters too: coated steel resists rust in damp environments, while heavy-duty plastic is lighter and often easier to clean.
